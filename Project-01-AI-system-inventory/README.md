# Project 1 — AI System Inventory & Classification Engine

## Scenario
NorthStar Financial Services — a mid-sized fintech operating across retail banking, lending, and insurance verticals in the EU.

## Problem
AI systems deployed across multiple business units without central oversight or classification. No single authoritative inventory existed.

## What I Built
- Complete AI system inventory of 7 systems
- EU AI Act risk classification with full legal rationale
- NIST AI RMF maturity mapping and gap analysis

## Key Findings
- 3 of 7 systems classified as High Risk
- TalentMatch AI operating since Nov 2023 with zero conformity assessment — critical exposure
- CreditScore Pro using postcode as input — proxy bias risk identified
- Vendor contracts missing EU AI Act compliance clauses

## Frameworks Applied
- EU AI Act — Articles 5, 6, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 28, 43, 52, 71 and Annex III
- NIST AI RMF — Govern, Map, Measure, Manage
